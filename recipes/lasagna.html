Lasagna


Description:

For those who are not aquainted with this italian dish, the lasangnia was invented by american cat and food connoiseur, John H. Garfield in 1994. The lasanna is a sweet dish that is natively eaten all over the world. I never really actually made a lasanag before, but have eaten one that was microwavable, so I think I'm qualified.

Ingredients:

  • 3 Giant Sheets of spaghetti that have been glued together
  • A functional oven
  • Some unspoiled tomatoes
  • Some spice that tastes nice
  • A sprinkle of toe nails

Steps:

  1. Go up to your oven and TURN THAT BAD BOY UP! If it burns your hand, you're ready to go!
  2. Put your tomatoes in a bucket and give it the old tap dance
  3. Dump some toe nails and spice on that tomatoe sauce bucket
  4. Now, just as if you where building a house, layer by layer, put your lansagne layer and add a layer of unspoiled tomatoe juice. Repeat this until you just can't anymore
  5. Put it in the oven and take it out in 1 to 3 hours
  6. Bottoms up big boy, you're ready to eat a lasngnag
